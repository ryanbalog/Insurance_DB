Providers: Doctors that have rendered service to a member
Policies: Coverage plans available for purchase
Policy Accounts: The parent entity that is linked to an insurance plan
Account Members: ID dependent weak entity for the Policy Account. These are people that are under the policy account.
Service: Information involving a policy holder or dependent's visit to a provider that is being billed to the insurance
Service Items: List of diagnostic and procedure codes from each service instance
ICD-10: Standardized diagnosis codes that are alphanumeric
CPT: Standardized procedure codes created by AMA. You must pay to have access to these codes, so I made them up for this project.
